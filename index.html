// About me

Design that converts

My name is João Lucas Santana, I am 23 years old, from Brazil, and I work as a thumbnail designer while pursuing a degree in Advertising.

My journey in design began in 2021, when I started looking for a way to make money online. Since childhood, I have always been connected to art, but my first contact with the world of thumbnails came when I tried to create my own YouTube channel. The channel did not work out, but during that process I discovered Photoshop and found my true field of work.

Over time, I became a professional graphic designer and specialized in creating strategic and visually impactful thumbnails focused on increasing my clients' video CTR.

Today, I understand that the thumbnail is the viewer's first contact with a video. In a feed full of content, standing out visually is essential. That is why I aim to combine strategy and aesthetics, delivering eye-catching designs that generate real results and strengthen each channel's visual identity.
